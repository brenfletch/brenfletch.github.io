Lincoln Financial Core Brochure
app

Client

Self

Services

Art direction and strategy

Team

Brenda Fletcher - Art director
Ken Velasquez – Designer

Date

2017

We created a beautifully designed brochure with clear data illustrations that was highly received in the industry. We are proud of the piece Lincoln Financial Group has added to their portfolio with this brochure.

Challenge

Lincoln Financial Group was launching a modern annuity product in collaboration with a partner company but lacked a compelling, growth-oriented communication tool. They needed more than a beautiful brochure—they needed a strategic asset that could drive engagement, inform stakeholders, and support acquisition and brand differentiation.

Strategy

I led both art direction and strategic planning to elevate the brochure beyond design:

  • Conducted competitive reviews to assess visual and messaging gaps in existing product communications.
  • Developed mood boards that informed a bold aesthetic direction aimed at modernizing the brand alongside product innovation.
  • Crafted impactful data visualizations and messaging that conveyed value clearly and persuasively, designed to engage advisors and prospects.
