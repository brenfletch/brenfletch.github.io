Good Market Mobile App

UX designer, Sketch, Illustrator, Invision

The Goal

Good Market's goal was to expand their customer base and market share. They wanted to design and release a pilot program in Philadelphia, iterate based on what they learn and then expand the product to other cities around the country.

The Process

I started the process with competitive anylasis, card sorting and with started laying out a site map. I then focused on how the mobile app would function and created several user flows such as creating accounts and purchasing.

The Outcome

The mobile app design, when user-tested, score very high in usability. The most common response was that it was simple to navigate, and the purchasing process and shopping cart was was easy understand. Steps are now being made for development.
